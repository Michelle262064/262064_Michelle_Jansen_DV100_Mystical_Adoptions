Adopt A Creature

Your journey with a little legend starts here.

Adopting a little legend doesn't cost a thing - our focus is finding loving homes. We want to make their arrival extra special, so you can choose optional starter packages made just for your creature: tiny beds, treats, toys, and more. You can even get multiple packages if your legend will have indoor and outdoor spaces. After you complete your application, we'll arrange a magical meeting so you can see which creature is the perfect fit. We've also included a beginners friendly rating with our creatures so that you can gauge if you are ready to make tehm apart of your family.
